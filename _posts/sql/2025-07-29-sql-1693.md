### 정답
```sql
SELECT
    date_id, make_name,
    COUNT(DISTINCT lead_id) unique_leads,
    COUNT(DISTINCT partner_id) unique_partners
FROM DailySales
GROUP BY date_id, make_name
```

#### ✅ Point
- `COUNT(lead_id)` 만으론 distinct 한 id의 개수를 구할 수 없음
- `COUNT(DISTINCT lead_id)` 로
