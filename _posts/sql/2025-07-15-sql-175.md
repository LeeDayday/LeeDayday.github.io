### 정답
```sql
SELECT P.firstName, P.lastName, A.city, A.state
FROM Person P
LEFT JOIN Address A
ON P.personId = A.personId

```

#### ✅ Point
- Address 가 없는 경우엔 null 처리 -> `LEFT JOIN`
